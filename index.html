Sardines Burger
Ingredients:
  • 3 cans | King Sardines in Tomato Sauce, drained
  • 2 tbsp | Oil
  • 3 tbsp | All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup | Bread crumbs
  • 3 tbsp | Milk
  • 1/2 pc | Egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup | Jicama, small, diced
  • 4 tbsp | Onion, small, diced
  • Pinch | Cumin
  • 1/8 pc | Broth cube
  • 2 tsp | Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 slices | Tomatoes
  • 4-6 pcs | Lettuce
  • 3-4 pcs | Cheese, sliced
  • 3-4 pcs | Burger buns
  • 6 tbsp | Cheese sauce, homemade
Procedure:
  1. Drain sauce from King Sardines in Tomato Sauce. Set aside.
  2. Sauté onions with Cumin, broth cube, and Worcestershire sauce until onions are translucent. Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, combine King Sardines, flour, bread crumbs, milk, egg, jicama, around four tbsp of the sauce from King Sardines, and sautéed onions. Mix well.
  4. Mold or shape an 80-100g of the mixture into a burger patty. It can be stored in a chillar(3-5 days shelf life), freezer(3-5 weeks life), or it can be cooked directly.
  5. Put oil in a pre-heated frying pan. Fry burger patty for 4-6 minutes each side or until brown. If necessary, put in the oven with the heat of 360°F until tender. Set aside.
  6. Assemble burger buns, catsup, burger patty, sliced cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes. Drizzle with cheese sauce.
  7. Put in a plate with fries or chips. Serve.
Lumpiang Shanghai Sardines
Ingredients:
  • 3 cans | King Sardines in Tomato Sauce, drained
  • 100 g | Shrimp, minced
  • 3 tbsp | Onion, small, diced
  • 3 tbsp | Carrots, small, diced
  • 1 tbsp | Cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp | Oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp | Egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp | All-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp | Bread crumbs
  • As needed | Salt
  • As needed | Pepper
  • 20 pcs | Shanghai wrapper
  • 1 cup | Catsup
Procedure:
  1. Drain sauce from King Sardines in Tomato Sauce . Set aside.
  2. Sauté onion, carrots, and Worcestershire sauce. Set aside.
  3. Combine drained sardines, shrimp, sautéed onion and carrots, cilantro, egg, flour, and breadcrumbs. Mix it well then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Wrap and roll mixture in a shanghai wrapper, then set aside. It can be stored in a freezer (3-5 weeks, shelf life) or it can be fried directly.
  5. Pre-heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat and fry lumpiang shanghai until all sides are golden brown. Drain in a rack or paper towels to remove excess oil.
Sardines Pasta
Ingredients:
  • 3 cans | King Sardines in Tomato Sauce
  • 300 g | Spaghetti pasta, uncooked
  • 1/4 cup | Oyster sauce
  • 1 pc | Pork cube
  • 4 tbsp | Onion, small, diced
  • 1 tbsp | Garlic, minced
  • 100 g | Cheddar cheese, grated
  • Pinch | Thyme
  • Pinch | Rosemary
  • To taste | Salt
  • To taste | Pepper
  • 3 tbsp | Sugar
  • Pinch | Parsley, chopped
Procedure:
  1. Put the spaghetti pasta in seasoned boiling water as per pasta instructions, or until al dente. Drain and then set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion, garlic, pork cube, thyme, and rosemary until onion is translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce, oyster sauce, and King Sardines in Tomato Sauce. Stir well until sardines are well-incorporated in the sauce. Add Cheddar cheese, stir again, then simmer until cheese is incorporated with the sauce. Season it with salt, pepper, and sugar.
  4. Next, add the cooked spaghetti pasta with the sauce. Mix it until pasta is coated well.
  5. Put it in a plate then garnish with cheese and parsley. Serve.
Sardines Pizza
Ingredients:
  • 3 cans | King Sardines in Tomato Sauce
  • 3 pcs | Pizza dough, pre-baked
  • 1 cup | Tomato sauce
  • 4 tbsp | Onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp | Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pc | Pork cube
  • 40 g | Mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 150 g | Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 50 g | Bacon bits
  • 3 tbsp | Sugar
  • To taste | Salt
  • To taste | Pepper
  • Pinch | Thyme
  • As needed | Parsley, chopped
Procedure:
  1. Pre-heat oven in 180°C or 355°F temperature.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, thyme, and pork cube until onion is translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce and King Sardines in Tomato Sauce. Stir and mix well until sardines are well-incorporated with the sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar, then set aside.
  4. Coat or spread pizza sauce on top of pizza dough, then add cheese and parsley. Put it in the pre-heated oven and bake for 8-12 minutes, or until top is well-browned.
  5. Garnish with bacon bits, slice evenly, then serve hot.
Tuna Ratatouille
Ingredients:
  • 1 can | King Sardines Tuna
  • 1/4 cup | Zucchini, sliced
  • 1/4 cup | Eggplant
  • 1/2 cup | Tomato, sliced
  • 1/3 cup | Olive oil
  • 1/2 small head | Onion
  • 1/4 pc | Bell pepper, diced
  • Pinch | Thyme
  • Pinch | Oregano
  • 1/3 cup | Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tbsp | Tomato Paste
  • To taste | Salt
  • To taste | Pepper
Procedure:
  1. In a pan, sauté onion, garlic, bellpepper,thyme and oregano. Then add tomato Sauce and tomato paste then cook sauce for 5-3 minutes. Set aside.
  2. In a tray, put zucchini, eggplant and tomatoes, season to taste. Drizzle it with olive oil then put it in 380-400F pre-heated oven for 5-10 minutes or until tender.
  3. Mix sauce to the roasted vegetables together with King Sardines Tuna.
  4. Put In a plate together with salad greens(optional).
  5. Serve ratatouille warm.
Tuna Nicoise
Procedure:
  • 1 can | King Sardines Tuna in Flakes
  • 50 g | French beans, blanched
  • 1 pc | Potato, roasted/boiled, wedges
  • 1 pc | Egg, hard boiled, sliced
  • 3-4 pcs | Olives, pitted, sliced
  • 2 cups | Salad Greens
  • 4 pcs | Cherry Tomatoes, quarters
  • 6 tbsp | Dijon Mustard
  • 3 tbsp | Olive Oil
  • 3 tbsp | Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/4 head | Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves | Garlic
  • Pinch | Oregano
  • Pinch | Thyme
  • To taste | Sugar
  • To taste | Salt
  • To taste | Pepper
Procedure:
  1. Boil French beans, potato and egg. Set aside.
  2. Make a vinaigrette, put mustard, olive oil, garlic, onion, vinegar, oregano and thyme in a food processor or a blender, then season with sugar, salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, put salad greens then toss it with vinaigrette. Put on plate.
  4. Add eggs, French beans, potatoes, olives cherry tomatoes and King Sardines Tuna in Flakes all over salad greens.
  5. Serve cold or room temperature.
Sardines Quiche
Procedure:
  • 2-3 cans | King Sardines Natural Oil Sardines
  • 1/3 cup | Quickmelt Cheese
  • 1/4 cup | Bacon
  • 2 tbsp | Onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup | Button Mushroom, sliced
  • 2 pcs | Egg
  • 1/3 cup | Cream, all purpose
  • 1/2 tsp | Salt
  • 1 cup | Flour
  • 1/4 cup | Butter, soften
  • 1/4 cup | Water, cold
Procedure:
  1. To make crust, in a bowl,mix four and salt. In a different bowl, whisk soft butter and water until well combined.
  2. In a bowl, combine flour and water mixture then mix using whisk or fork until crumby.
  3. Put dough in a pie pan then bake it at 400°F until golden brown.
  4. Set aside crust and let It rest.
  5. In a pan, render bacon until bacon oil is produced, then add onion, mushroom, a bit of King Sardines and King Sardines brine until reduced. Set aside and let it cool.
  6. Mix egg, cheese and cream until well incorporated, then mix with bacon and sardine mixture. Mix well.
  7. Put egg mixture on crust then bake at 300*F around 30-45 minutes or until mixture does not wiggle anymore.
  8. Put the rest of King Sardines, cheese and tomatoes on top then bake until top is well browned.
  9. Remove from pan then serve hot.
Sardines Submarine
Procedure:
  • 1 can | King Sardines in Natural Oil
  • 1 long piece | Baguette
  • 1 pc | Tomatoes, sliced
  • As needed | Salad Greens
  • 1/2 pc | Onions, sliced
  • 2 slices | Cheddar cheese
  • 2-3 pcs | Bacon, strips
  • 3 tbsp | Mayonaisse
  • 1 clove | Garlic
  • Pinch | Paprika
  • Drops | Lemons, juice
  • To taste | Salt
  • To taste | Pepper
  • To taste | Sugar
Procedure:
  1. In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, garlic, paprika, lemon juice and a bit of sardines brine to taste. Season with salt, pepper and sugar. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, render bacon until crisp. Set aside.
  3. Slice baguette, half lengthwise, then spread garlic mayonnaise inside baguette.
  4. Assemble salad greens, tomato, onions, bacon, cheese and LIGO Sardines in Natural Oil Inside the baguette.
  5. Put sandwich In oven to melt cheese. Take It out of the oven.
  6. Serve while sandwich is warm.
Tuna Tacos
Procedure:
  • 3 pcs | Small taco shells
  • 3 pcs | Lettuce
  • 1 can | King Sardines Tuna flakes in oil
  • 2 tbsp | Onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp | Tomato, deseeded, chopped
  • 1 tbsp | Jalapeno, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp | Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tsp | Lime Juice
  • 1 tsp | Honey
  • Pinch | Chill flakes
  • To taste | Salt and Pepper
Procedure:
  1. In a small bowl, drain King Sardines Tuna Flakes In Oil then mix with onion, tomato, pineapple, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, honey, and chili flakes. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place the lettuce In the taco shell.
  3. Fill the shell with spoonful of King Sardines Tuna mixture
  4. Serve with hot sauce If desired.
